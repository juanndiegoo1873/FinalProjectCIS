Project Website Plan: Kitchen Bites

Overview of Website

Recipe Hub is a social cooking website where users can create accounts to post their favorite food recipes, upvote or downvote othersâ€™ recipes, and share them on social media. The goal is to build a online space for people to discover and share meals.

Target Audience

The primary audience is everyday home cooks, new cooks, and seasoned cooks with experience, aged 16+. Also people seeking quick, reliable meals.

Planned Site Pages

  • Home Page: Trending recipes, newest posts, top-rated meals and a search bar.
  • Login/Create Account: User account registration and login system.
  • Submit a Recipe: Form page for users to post a new recipe with title, ingredients, steps, and an image.
  • Browse Recipes: Searchable list of all recipes, sortable by rating or date.
  • Recipe Detail Page: Individual recipe page with vote buttons, share links, and comments.

Visual Design

The site will be simple and clear. It will use warm and inviting colors mimicking a home feelings, which is in theme of what the website is. A simple font will be used. Layout will be minimal and organized, using headings and spacing to separate sections. This plain layout ensures the content stands out without distractions.

SEO Research

Keywords: recipes, cooking, home cooking, share recipes, easy meals, recipe website, food blog, cooking community, recipe ideas, post your recipe

Top similar sites:

Enhancing Viewer Experience

The site will be mobile-friendly and responsive. The layout will adjust for phones, tablets, and desktops. Easy navigation, fast loading, and large clickable areas will help users engage with recipes. Descriptive text, headings, and meta information will improve accessibility and search visibility.
